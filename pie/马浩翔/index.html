Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie











  • One 9-inch pie
  • 30 Min - Prep time
  • 10 Min - Cook time
  • 40 Min - Total
  • 8 Servings
    • INGREDIENTS
    • * 1 cup white sugar
    • * 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    • * 3 tablespoons cornstarch
    • * 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • * 1 1/2 cups water
    • * 2 lemons, juiced and zested
    • * 2 tablespoons butter
    • * 4 egg yolks, beaten
    • * 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
    • * 4 egg whites
    • * 6 tablespoons white sugar
